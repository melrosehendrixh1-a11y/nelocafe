Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our pricing and services.

What's included in the monthly fee?

Your monthly fee includes website hosting, domain management, security updates, performance monitoring, and ongoing technical support. It also covers regular backups and any minor content updates you need.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade your package at any time to access additional features and functionality. We'll handle the migration seamlessly without any downtime to your website.

Do you offer custom packages?

Yes, we can create custom packages tailored to your specific business needs. Contact us to discuss your requirements and we'll provide a personalized solution that fits your budget and goals.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, EFT transfers, and PayPal. For annual packages, we also offer payment plans to make it easier for your business cash flow.
